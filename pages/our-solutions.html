Our Industrial Solutions

Discover our energy-saving industrial thermal curtains designed for maximum efficiency and operational flexibility.

Features & Benefits

Reduce Heat Loss & Save Energy

Our industrial thermal curtains are a highly effective solution for conserving energy and significantly reducing heating and cooling expenditures in large industrial spaces. By creating distinct temperature-controlled zones, they minimize thermal transfer and directly reduce the load on HVAC systems.

Flexible Zoning & Control

Our curtains are designed to create flexible, controlled environments for various applications, from cold storage to sensitive manufacturing processes. This allows you to effectively manage climate control in specific areas without altering the entire facility.

Fast, Flexible Installation

ClimateCurtains are a cost-effective alternative to traditional fixed walls. They are generally less expensive to acquire, quicker to install, and can be easily removed or reconfigured as operational needs change, offering unparalleled adaptability for any facility.

Bespoke Design & Function

Our curtains are made-to-measure to ensure maximum functionality and a perfect fit for any industrial opening or space. We offer various customization options such as valances, Velcro or magnet seals, and strip doors to meet your specific requirements.

Our Technology: The Patented ClimateCurtain® System

The ClimateCurtain® is a bespoke roller blind system specifically engineered for thermal insulation. The core principle is the creation of a sealed, insulating air pocket between the window pane and the curtain fabric. Each system is custom-manufactured to ensure a precise, airtight fit, maximizing the integrity of this thermal barrier. Our unique technology is protected by Swedish patent SE540848C2, providing a strong, defensible advantage against competitors.

Key Differentiators & Competitive Advantage

The ClimateCurtain® system offers a combination of performance, cost-effectiveness, and flexibility that competing solutions cannot match.

  • Superior Thermal Performance: Unlike PVC strip curtains designed for dust control, our system is engineered as a high-performance thermal barrier from the ground up.
  • Non-Invasive Installation: Our system can be installed without any structural renovations or drilling. This makes it the only viable solution for historically listed buildings and avoids costly downtime in active industrial facilities. This is particularly valuable in markets like Japan, where minimizing operational disruption is critical for facilities using just-in-time principles.
  • Bespoke Design: Every ClimateCurtain® is made-to-measure, ensuring a perfect seal and seamless aesthetic integration. The system also incorporates a complete blackout function, essential for facilities requiring precise light control like data centers or research labs.
