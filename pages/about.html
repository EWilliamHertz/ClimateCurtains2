About ClimateCurtainsAB

Dedicated to sustainable innovation and industrial energy efficiency.

Our Story

ClimateCurtainsAB is a Swedish company founded with the mission to provide innovative, energy-saving solutions for industrial environments. Our core product, industrial thermal curtains, addresses the significant energy waste challenges faced by warehouses, production facilities, and other large-scale operations.

Led by our CEO, Peter Hertz, we are committed to helping businesses reduce their operational costs and environmental footprint by enhancing thermal efficiency.

Our Vision & Mission

Our vision is to provide the opportunity for significant energy and cost savings for industrial enterprises worldwide. Our mission is to:

  • Develop and deliver high-quality, durable industrial thermal curtains.
  • Empower businesses to enhance operational efficiency through improved thermal zoning.
  • Contribute to global sustainability goals by providing tangible solutions for energy reduction.
