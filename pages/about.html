About ClimateCurtainsAB

Dedicated to sustainable innovation and industrial energy efficiency.

Our Journey of Innovation

Founded by inventor Peter Hertz, ClimateCurtainsAB was born from a mission to solve a critical inefficiency in industrial buildings. Our journey has been marked by key milestones that validate our technology and vision:

  • 2018: After years of R&D, we were awarded the prestigious Energy Globe Award for Sweden, recognizing our technology as a world-class sustainable innovation among submissions from over 180 countries.
  • August 2018: We officially launched our first commercial product in Färgelanda, Sweden, transitioning from a concept to a market-ready solution.
  • August 2019: Our technology was selected for the high-profile "Zero Island" sustainability project on Lidö, proving its real-world effectiveness and contributing to the project's remarkable 78% reduction in emissions.

Our Team

Peter Hertz

Founder & CEO

Peter Hertz, Founder & CEO: Mr. Hertz is the visionary inventor behind the core patented technology of the ClimateCurtain® system. With a deep understanding of thermodynamics and building physics, he identified the critical inefficiency of industrial windows and dedicated himself to creating an elegant, effective, and commercially viable solution. His leadership provides the company with an unparalleled depth of product expertise and an unwavering commitment to quality and innovation.
