Proven Impact: The "Zero Island" Project

A pivotal moment in our company's history was our selection as a key technology partner for the "Zero Island" project on Lidö in the Stockholm archipelago. This ambitious initiative aimed to transform the island into a completely fossil-free environment.

ClimateCurtainsAB installed twenty of our units across the island, which were integral to the project's success. The initiative achieved a remarkable 78% reduction in total emissions, validating our product's performance in a demanding, real-world application and aligning our brand with pioneering environmentalism.

Independent Industry Recognition

Further validating our impact, ClimateCurtainsAB was named the national winner for Sweden in the 2018 Energy Globe Awards. This is one of the most prestigious honors in sustainability, providing objective, international confirmation of our technology's novelty and significant potential for positive environmental impact.

Mathematical Relationships & Formulas

Understanding the science behind our energy savings calculations.

Key Mathematical Relationships

Energy Savings per Square Meter

Formula: Energy Savings ($kWh/m^2$) = Total Annual kWh Saved $\div$ Curtain Area ($m^2$)

CO2 Reduction Calculation

Formula: CO2 Reduced (tons) = Energy Saved (kWh) $\times$ 0.0001 tons/kWh

Payback Period Estimation

Formula: Payback (months) = (Installation Cost $\div$ Annual Savings) $\times$ 12
