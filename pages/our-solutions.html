Our Industrial Solutions

Discover our patented, energy-saving industrial thermal curtains, engineered for maximum efficiency, regulatory compliance, and operational flexibility.

Features & Benefits

Reduce Heat Loss & Save Energy

Our industrial thermal curtains are a highly effective solution for conserving energy and significantly reducing heating and cooling expenditures. By creating distinct temperature-controlled zones, they minimize thermal transfer, directly reducing the load on HVAC systems and helping you meet and exceed energy efficiency mandates.

Flexible Zoning & Control

Our curtains are designed to create flexible, controlled environments for diverse applications, from cold storage and data centers to sensitive manufacturing processes. This allows you to effectively manage climate control in specific areas without altering the entire facility, optimizing energy use where it's needed most.

Fast, Non-Invasive Installation

ClimateCurtains are a cost-effective alternative to fixed walls. They are quicker to install and can be easily removed or reconfigured as operational needs change. Our non-invasive process avoids costly downtime, making it the only viable solution for historically listed buildings and active 24/7 facilities.

Bespoke Design & Function

Our curtains are made-to-measure to ensure a perfect, airtight fit for any industrial opening or space. We offer customization options like complete blackout functionality, essential for data centers or research labs requiring precise environmental and light control.

Award-Winning & Field-Validated Technology

Our solutions are not just theoretical; they are proven in the real world and recognized by international experts.

  • Energy Globe Award Winner: Our patented technology was selected as the national winner for Sweden, a prestigious honor that validates the innovation and environmental impact of our ClimateCurtain® system.
  • Proven in the "Zero Island" Project: Deployed in a high-profile sustainability initiative, our curtains were a key factor in achieving a 78% reduction in CO2 emissions, demonstrating their effectiveness in demanding, real-world conditions.

Key Differentiators & Competitive Advantage

The ClimateCurtain® system offers a combination of performance, cost-effectiveness, and flexibility that competing solutions cannot match.

  • Superior Thermal Performance: Unlike common PVC strip curtains designed for dust control, our system is engineered as a high-performance thermal barrier, delivering results comparable to capital-intensive renovations at a fraction of the cost.
  • Non-Invasive Installation: Our system can be installed without any structural renovations or drilling. This makes it the only viable solution for historically listed buildings and avoids costly downtime in active industrial facilities. This is particularly valuable in markets like Japan, where minimizing operational disruption is critical.
  • Bespoke Design & Blackout Functionality: Every ClimateCurtain® is made-to-measure, ensuring a perfect seal and seamless aesthetic integration. The system also incorporates a complete blackout function, essential for facilities requiring precise light control like data centers or research labs.
