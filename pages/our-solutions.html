ClimateCurtains Overview

Our industrial thermal curtains are a flexible and cost-effective alternative to rigid, fixed walls for temperature control within large spaces. They are built from durable materials to withstand demanding environments.

Material Specifications

Outer layers are made from durable 13oz or 18oz vinyl reinforced with polyester scrim. The inner insulation is polyfiber batting stitched in place to prevent settling. These materials make our curtains waterproof, easy to clean, and suitable for temperatures as low as -28°C.

R-Value and Thermal Performance

Our curtains are available with a range of R-values, from R3 to R9 for standard applications and up to R15 for stationary installations. A higher R-value signifies greater insulating power, allowing clients to select the appropriate level of insulation for their specific needs.

Customization Options

  • Valances: To close the gaps at the top of the curtain.
  • Velcro or Magnet Seals: To ensure a tight seal between curtain sections or against walls.
  • Strip Doors: For easy passage of foot traffic or motorized traffic.
  • Power Roll-up Doors: For automated, high-speed access through the curtain barrier.

These options allow for a tailored solution that meets the specific operational needs of any industrial environment.

Application Examples

Industrial & Production Facilities

Our curtains are designed to create temperature-controlled zones in large warehouses, cold storage facilities, loading docks, and manufacturing plants. They reduce energy waste and optimize process control by improving the thermal envelope.

Hospitality & Large Venues

Our curtains can be used in large restaurants and hotel common areas to segment spaces and create flexible temperature zones. They help maintain guest comfort and reduce the load on central HVAC systems, leading to energy and cost savings.
